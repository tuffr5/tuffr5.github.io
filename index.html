About Me

Hi! This is Bin Duan. I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Texas State University, under the supervision of Dr. Yan Yan. I received my BS degree in EE from LanZhou University, China in 2017. My research interests are machine learning, graph neural network, deep representation learning, robotics and their applications to computer vision.
