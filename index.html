Bin Duan

Multimodal representation learning and scalable systems for scientific data

Postdoctoral Research Fellow, University of Michigan Medical School

duanb.at.umich.edu · Google Scholar · GitHub · CV (PDF)

Research

I build the algorithmic and computational infrastructure that makes large multimodal data usable for discovery, developing representation learning frameworks and open systems that align, register, and synthesize structure across imaging and related modalities. Central question: How can learning algorithms and infrastructure be co-designed to turn complex data into interpretable and actionable insight for scientific discovery?

Multimodal representation learning Registration and structure-aware modeling Neural compression and fidelity Uncertainty and reliability Open, scalable platforms
