About Me

Hi! This is Bin Duan. I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science at Illinois Tech, under the supervision of Dr. Yan Yan. I received my BS degree in EE from LanZhou University, China in 2017. I mostly work on applications on biomedical images, plus some interest on generic modeling of different learning problems.
